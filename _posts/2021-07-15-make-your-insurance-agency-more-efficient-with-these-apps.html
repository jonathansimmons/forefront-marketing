The earliest adopters of new technologies usually have a massive advantage over those who wait, but adopting new tech takes time and energy. Today, we’re here to share a few of our favorite productivity apps and tips for how to focus on what actually improves your day-to-day workload.

Favorite Productivity Apps for Insurance Agents

There’s a good chance you already tried new ways to get the job done and it was terrible (or at least bad enough to make you not want to try again). We understand. As an independent agent, your priority is the customer, but you’re also trying to keep up with the industry.

If you’re afraid to adopt new stuff, start small. Are you the type of person who 1) loves staying up to date with current trends but 2) doesn’t have time to sit around and read a bunch of articles? Try an app like Pocket, which helps you collect to-read content and then read it at your leisure. You can even choose to listen to the article instead, if you’re more of a podcast fan.

Or if you just need to get more organized but you’re not looking to reinvent the wheel, try productivity apps like Todoist or Evernote. These apps centralize your schedule and information.
