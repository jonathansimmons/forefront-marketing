Objective

Developer Adeption approached Fourfront about two projects in late 2020. The first was a video for their client Walmart, to use in support of a training module Adeption was developing. The second was a series of shorts for a platform they were developing for another client, Medtronic. Both needed to be executed remotely, without any in-person work because of pandemic-related constraints.

Solution

Fourfront’s work on the Walmart video— finalizing content, coordinating production, and animating— was so well-received, that the Walmart leadership team repurposed the video to be used in training modules for leadership company-wide.

For the Medtronic project, Fourfront assembled audio kits and prepared a detailed instructional video, empowering company executives to operate their own recording equipment with minimal effort while the interviews were conducted via Zoom. In all, 10 modules were delivered for use in the content platform, featuring an original brand and a dynamic motion graphics package.
